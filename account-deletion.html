Account Deletion - Mane

Last updated: June 2026

Mane does not currently maintain a separate server-side user account database. If you sign in with Google, basic sign-in information may be stored locally on your device to keep you signed in and recognize premium access.

How to delete your account data

To remove Mane data from your device, uninstall the app or clear the app's data from your Android device settings.

You may also contact us at somnrun@gmail.com if you have questions about account deletion.

What is deleted

  • Locally stored app data can be deleted by uninstalling Mane or clearing app data.
  • This may include saved words, learning progress, settings, recent searches, and locally stored sign-in state.

What may be retained

  • Google Play purchase and subscription records are managed by Google Play.
  • Some records may be retained by Google or payment processors according to their own policies.
