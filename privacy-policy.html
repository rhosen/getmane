Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At a glance

  1. Learning data stays on your device
  2. Google sign-in may store basic account info locally
  3. Google Play manages premium billing
  4. AdMob and Crashlytics may process diagnostics data

Overview

Mane respects your privacy and aims to collect only the data needed to provide learning, premium, and support features.

The app stores learning-related data on your device, including saved words, learning progress, practice activity, settings, and recent search history. This data is used to keep features working and personalize your experience.

Sign-in

If you sign in with Google, the app may store basic account details on your device, such as your email address, display name, profile photo, and account identifier, so your premium access and signed-in state can be recognized.

Premium, ads, and diagnostics

Premium purchases are handled through Google Play Billing. Purchase status and entitlement information may be processed to activate, restore, and manage premium access.

Ads may be provided through Google AdMob. AdMob and related Google services may collect device, advertising, and diagnostics information under their own policies.

If crash reporting is enabled, Firebase Crashlytics may collect crash and diagnostics information to help improve app stability. Mane does not intentionally include searched words, translation text, or other user-entered learning content in crash reports.

Your choices

Mane does not sell your personal information. Third-party services used by the app operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

You can clear recent search history from Settings, and learning progress or other locally stored app data remains on your device until you remove it or reset it through available app options.
